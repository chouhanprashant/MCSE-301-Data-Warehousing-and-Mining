# Genetic Algorithm – Very Simple Explanation (Exam-Pass Level)

## Definition
Genetic Algorithm ek technique hai jo
**best solution dhundhne** ke liye
**natural evolution** ka idea use karti hai.

Simple words:
> Genetic Algorithm “survival of the fittest” par kaam karta hai.

---

## Why Genetic Algorithm?
Kuch problems me:
- Bahut saare possible solutions hote hain
- Best solution directly milna mushkil hota hai

Genetic Algorithm:
- Dheere-dheere better solution tak pahunchta hai

---

## Core Idea
> Jo solution achha hota hai, wahi aage continue hota hai.

---

## Main Steps of Genetic Algorithm

1. **Population**
   - Possible solutions ka group

2. **Fitness Check**
   - Kaunsa solution achha hai, ye check karna

3. **Selection**
   - Best solutions choose karna

4. **Crossover**
   - Do solutions ko mix karna

5. **Mutation**
   - Chhota random change karna

6. **New Generation**
   - Naye aur better solutions milte hain

---

## Simple Flow
Population  
↓  
Fitness Check  
↓  
Selection  
↓  
Crossover  
↓  
Mutation  
↓  
Best Solution  

---

## Use of Genetic Algorithm in Data Mining
- Feature selection
- Optimization
- Rule generation

---

## Advantages
- Complex problems solve karta hai
- Global best solution dhundhta hai
- Flexible technique

---

## Limitations
- Time zyada lag sakta hai
- Exact answer guarantee nahi

---

## Exam Trigger Line
Genetic Algorithm is inspired by natural evolution and follows survival of the fittest.
