## WHAT (Data Mining kya hai?)
**Data Mining** ka matlab hai:
> Large amount of data me se **useful patterns, relationships, trends aur knowledge** ko discover karna.

### Simple definition (exam-friendly):
Data Mining is the process of extracting **meaningful information and knowledge** from large databases using statistical, machine learning and AI techniques.

---

## HOW (Data Mining karta kya hai?)
Data Mining mainly ye kaam karta hai:
- Classification (data ko categories me divide karna)
- Clustering (similar data ko group karna)
- Association (items ke beech relation nikalna)
- Prediction (future trend batana)
- Outlier detection (abnormal data pakadna)

---

## WHERE (Data Mining kaha use hota hai?)
- Banking (fraud detection)
- Retail (market basket analysis)
- Healthcare (disease prediction)
- Web mining (recommendation systems)
- Telecom & security
