## Data Mining
Data Mining ka matlab hai:
large data se **useful patterns aur knowledge nikalna**.

Use:
- Decision making
- Prediction
- Pattern discovery

---

## KDD Process
KDD = Knowledge Discovery in Databases  

Steps:
- Data selection
- Data preprocessing
- Data mining
- Pattern evaluation
- Knowledge

---

## Data Preprocessing
Raw data ko **clean aur ready banana**.

Steps:
- Cleaning
- Integration
- Selection
- Transformation
- Reduction

---

## Association Rule Mining
Items ke beech **relationship** nikalta hai.

Example:
Agar bread liya → butter bhi liya

Measures:
- Support
- Confidence

---

## Apriori Algorithm
Frequent itemsets nikalne ka algorithm.

Idea:
- Chhote frequent → bade frequent
- Candidate generation + pruning

(Numerical yahin aata hai)

---

## FP-Growth
Frequent itemsets bina candidate banaye nikalta hai.

Steps:
- FP-Tree banana
- Conditional pattern base
- Frequent itemsets

Fast algorithm.

---

## Clustering
Similar data ko **group** karna.

Type:
- Unsupervised

Use:
- Customer segmentation
- Image grouping

---

## K-Means
Cluster center = **mean (average)**  
Fast but outliers se affect hota hai.

---

## K-Medoids
Cluster center = **actual data point**  
Outliers handle karta hai.

---

## BIRCH

Idea:
- CF-Tree use karta hai
- Data compress karke cluster karta hai

---

- Leaf node
